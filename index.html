Welcome to Armbarn

Modernize your Pitch Charting Experience

Switch between collecting data and viewing statistics at your discretion. Change pitchers without starting a new chart. Export statistic reports to share with others or save it for future reference.

Make the right call

Follow the on screen prompts to enter every action. Just enter the information being requested and everything else takes care of itself.

Enter something incorrectly? No problem, either undo the previous pitch or just go back to the prior prompt for re-entry.

Who's on first?

Never lose track what has been recorded. The scoreboard reflects the current state of the game, giving the user feedback about what was previously entered. Both the pitch count and at-bat numbers can be hidden by tapping on either value.

Juiced Analytics

Know everything about the game being recorded, whether its on an individual or team level. The game report gives statistics relating to the currently selected player. Any player's stats can be viewed for the current game by selecting them as the active player. An overview of the whole game is also available, giving information on a pitch to pitch basis.
